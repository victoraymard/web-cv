Discovering the IoT

During my semester in Sweden, I was introduced to the IOT through a home automation project, bringing together numerous technologies within a complete project. This one aiming to interact from a smartphone, with Philips Hue lamps, via beacons and a cloud.

From sensor to measurement

Armed with an Arduino board and an unknown photosensitive sensor, the objective of this project was to decipher a coded message using light signals. This project required a rigorous analysis of the datasheet of the sensor in order to successfully use it to unravel the mystery of the secret message.
take a look at the report

Containment and arcade bollard

For entertainment during confinement, there's nothing like a connected arcade game!
Based on the famous game « Whac-A-Mole », the aim of this project was to recode this reflex game on an ESP32 card, then to connect it thanks to to the MQTT protocol so that he can compare his score with his friends' by trying to beat the better.
take a look at the report

Towards augmented reality

This project of introduction to man-machine interfaces has allowed me to try to modernise the visits which seem to me today to be outdated. For this purpose I have developed my own Augmented reality maps with the means available at the time of containment.
